Testimonials

"ClarityCCM employs the AHRQ’s Patient-Centered Team-Based Care* model in partnering with Primary Care Practices to comprehensively provide the full range of chronic care and preventive care services. This is achieved utilizing Clarity CCMÔ-employed staff under both general and direct supervision by the provider, as well as integrative software solutions, at no expense to the practice."

"We help practices navigate the complexities and compliantly fulfill the requirements for providing a variety of ancillary patient services, including Chronic Care Management (CCM), Annual Wellness Visits (AWV), Advance Care Planning (ACP), Remote Physiologic Monitoring (RPM) and Transitional Care Management (TCM). In fact, it is our belief that the combined delivery of these services is the most efficient and beneficial approach, for both patients and providers. This is especially true if the plan is developed and implemented using the AWV, with its inherent health risk assessment (HRA) and 5-Year Plan-of-Care as the foundation."
